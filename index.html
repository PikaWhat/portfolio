Places to visit

Even if you are residing for a short time, you should take a look at the following places:

  • Tempelhoferfeld

    A former airport turned public park, perfect for cycling, picnicking, and enjoying outdoor activities.
    It’s a unique spot where you can experience the vast open space in the city.

  • Reichstag building

    The seat of the German Parliament, known for its stunning glass dome which offers panoramic views of the city.
    Be sure to book a visit in advance to enjoy the rooftop terrace.

  • Flohmarkt

    A vibrant flea market where you can find antiques, vintage items, and delicious street food.
    It's a great place to experience local culture and hunt for unique souvenirs.

Landmarks

Landmarks in Berlin are essential and speak about this historical city!

  • Brandenburger Tor

    The Brandenburg Gate is a neoclassical monument and one of Berlin's most famous landmarks, symbolizing the reunification of Germany.

  • Berlin Cathedral

    A magnificent Protestant cathedral known for its stunning architecture and beautiful dome.
    Visitors can climb to the top for a breathtaking view of the city.
